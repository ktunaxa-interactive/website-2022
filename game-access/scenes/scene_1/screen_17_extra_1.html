[Kulilu speaking]
You know how kiʾsuʾk kyukyit literally means 'is it a good day' right? And we use it to say hello. The other greetings work the same way. Kiʾsuʾk wiⱡnam means 'is it a good morning' and we use it to say good morning, and so on...

kiʾsuʾk kyukyit (Hello! / Good day!)

kiʾsuʾk wiⱡnam (Good morning!)

kiʾsuʾk kuⱡyukyit (Good afternoon!)

kiʾsuʾk kwaⱡkwayit (Good evening!)

kiʾsuʾk ȼiⱡmiyit (Good night!)
